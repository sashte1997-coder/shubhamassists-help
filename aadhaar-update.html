How to Update Your Aadhaar Card Online (Step-by-Step Guide)

Last Updated: July 2026

Need to update your mobile number, address, or other details in your Aadhaar Card? This guide explains the general process and the documents you may need before starting.

Why Update Your Aadhaar?

  • Change your address after moving.
  • Correct spelling mistakes.
  • Update your mobile number.
  • Keep your Aadhaar information current.

Documents You May Need

  • Identity Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Registered Mobile Number (if applicable)

Steps

  1. Visit the official Aadhaar service portal.
  2. Sign in or choose the appropriate update option.
  3. Select the detail you want to update.
  4. Upload the required documents if requested.
  5. Review all information carefully.
  6. Submit your request.
  7. Save the acknowledgement number for tracking.

Common Mistakes

  • Uploading blurred documents.
  • Entering incorrect personal information.
  • Using an inactive mobile number.
Helpful Tip:
Double-check all information before submitting your request to avoid delays.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I update my Aadhaar online?
Some updates can be completed online, while others may require a visit to an authorized Aadhaar centre.

How long does the update take?
Processing times can vary depending on the type of update and verification.
